About Us

Astral Expeditions has always been inspired by the past and present. We are deeply inspired by NASA history, especially the bravery of NASA astronauts, while keeping our sights on the cosmos. We’re passionate about bringing world-class space travel experiences to our passengers while pushing the boundaries of space travel.

Our Location

The Astral Expeditions Launch Center is located in Cape Canaveral, Florida - the historic launch site of NASA’s Mercury, Gemini, Apollo, and Shuttle Program missions.
